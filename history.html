History

ITB is one of the many institutes in Dublin. ITB specifically focus on educational fields such as Multimedia, Engineering, Computing, Child Care Studies, Languages, Horiculture, Applied Social Studies, Business and Community & Youth Development. In 2018, It was announced that ITB, ITT & DIT would combine as the first Technogical University in Dublin.This enchances bachelor's Degrees provided in the college to University's degrees.

Heritage

During the 1800's and 1900's. Where the college is today was considered farmland were very few would live in small circular homes out across the fields. There's been many diggings of skeletons and rumble of old buildings and people found around the Corduff area. A number of forts around the area also, caved in along with chapels too. Until now the area was very rural still to Dublin and Blanchardstown Village.
