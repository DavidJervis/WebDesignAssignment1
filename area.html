ITB's Area Impact

I included this article/extra page because I feel this subject has a factor or an impact on graduated students out of ITB and that is job opportunities in the area with numerous industrial estates and off course Blanchardstown Shopping Center. These local areas have a big impact with influence for jobs and work opportunites. I've noticed over my travels to college that the companies situated in the local area like Ebay and Vodafone. Now where I am in college is 2nd year and I haven't seen much promotion from these businesses yet in the college but it would be a fantastic opportunity for both the company and the college. I'm just being aware that once you graduate that the opportunity is still right at the door.

It's an opportunity to good to pass for my view, It would be great to see an influence on campus of local businesses because wouldn't they want graduates like us, new creativity. I hope especially with the TU upgrade ITB will have this becomes a factor.
